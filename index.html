A Taste of Nepal's Heart

Nestled in the warm streets of Attariya, Kutiro Restaurant is more than a place to eat — it's where tradition, warmth, and flavour come together in every bite.

From hand-rolled tandoori roti to sizzling sekuwa and fragrant biryani, our kitchen honours the authentic soul of Nepali cooking, made with spices sourced close to home.

Whether you're joining us for a quiet meal, a birthday celebration, or a family thali — every table at Kutiro is treated like family.
